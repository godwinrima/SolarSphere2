Discover the benefits of solar panels and how the can transform your energy usage

monocrystalline solar panel

This is due to their high efficiency and the quality of silicon used in their construction. Monocrystalline solar panels can still produce large amounts of electricity even in indirect or diffuse sunlight, making them a reliable choice in areas where climate change or shading is an issue. The average solar panel has a power output rating of 250 to 400 watts (W) and generates around 1.5 kilowatt-hours (kWh) of energy per day
